Calendar Sync

Paste your public iCal URL. Events in the feed will be imported as available dates.

How to get your iCal URL

Google Calendar: Open Google Calendar → click ⋮ next to your calendar → Settings → scroll to "Integrate calendar" → copy Secret address in iCal format.

Apple iCloud: Open Calendar → right-click your calendar → Share Calendar → enable Public Calendar → copy the link, then replace webcal:// with https://.

Outlook: Outlook.com → Calendar → Settings → View all Outlook settings → Calendar → Shared calendars → Publish to web → copy the ICS link.
